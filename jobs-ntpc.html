It has come to our notice that, fake interview calls, through e-mails, have been sent to individuals regarding job openings in NTPC Limited. Through these fraudulent mails, candidates are being asked to deposit refundable fee in bank accounts. It is clarified that NTPC Limited has never sent any such e-mails seeking refundable deposits for any job opening. All executive positions in NTPC are widely publicized across India through national newspapers and any communication related to employment is sent through post or official e-mail IDs of NTPC employees.

NTPC does not take any responsibility for candidates responding/ applying to such fake posts.
